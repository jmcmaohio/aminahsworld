Aminah Brenda Lynn Robinson

Combining traditional art materials with found objects and everyday materials such as buttons, cloth, leather, twigs, shells, and music box workings, Columbus, Ohio, artist Aminah Brenda Lynn Robinson (1940-2015) created magical two- and three-dimensional works of art. Welcome to Aminah's World where you can take a peek into the mind, work, and processes of this inspiring artist.

Aminah Robinson's work is a highlight of the collections of the Columbus Museum of Art, which is pleased to present this Website. For questions and comments, contact Deidre Hamlar, Director of the Aminah Robinson Legacy Project, 614-221-6801.
